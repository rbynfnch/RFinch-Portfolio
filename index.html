About Me

Super Lucky, is how I feel as a wife to an insiring husband and a mother of 5 amazing kids. I can’t help but share their love for technology and all things techie. Or maybe it’s the other way around and I have managed to pass on my fascination with technology and desire to know how to use it well. I have been teaching myself for 15 years how to build websites and do graphic design. I love creating and seeing the finished product. I love learning and continually strengthening my skills. My most recent learning endeavor has been to complete a 6 month Full-stack coding boot camp to be able to not only design websites on the front end but also from the back end. I have found this education to be exciting as the possibilities of the things I can create has really broadened. The possibilities are endless, technology is changing at a rapid pace, and I have no desire to slow down.
